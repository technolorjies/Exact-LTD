About
Exact Services LTD

EXACT SERVICES (ESL) is a private limited liability company incorporated with Corporate Affairs Commission. ESL is one of the fast upcoming and integrated engineering, procurement, construction, maintenance, and oil and gas services company. We have the capability of executing all engineering service works, covering engineering, procurement, construction, and commissioning to mention few.

We have participated in the execution of several engineering projects with major federal government agencies in Nigeria.ESL is focused on quality, cost-effective, innovative and cutting-edge solutions designed to meet the present day challenges of the Oil and Gas industry and other industrial markets in Nigeria.

With a well grounded team of experience Engineers and other technical staff involved in oil and gas services, our clients are assured to expect the level of expertise they required.

Our experience in the field of engineering maintenance as well as Oil and Gas industry enhance our performance and ability to meet our client expectation on time.
